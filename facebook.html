History

Facebook was founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates. It started as a social networking site for Harvard students and quickly expanded to other universities and eventually to the general public.

Privacy Settings

Facebook offers various privacy settings to control who can see your posts and personal information. Users can customize their privacy settings in their account settings.
